Journie

Journie is a self-improvement and mental health app equipped with a chatbot that invites introspective conversations and intrinsic motivation for growth. Using smart notifications and unpredictable messages, Journie encourages users to come back.

As this is my first app, a lot had to be learned. I designed and redesigned the app multiple times and it went through important stages to create the chatbot, activity quiz, history system, and smart notifications before I could settle with a project I love.
